Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1 Tasse Mehl
  • 2 Esslöffel Zucker
  • 1 Teelöffel Backpulver
  • 1/2 Teelöffel Salz
  • 1 Tasse Milch
  • 1 Ei
  • 2 Esslöffel Butter, geschmolzen

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  3. Add the minced beef to the skillet and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes and oregano. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
